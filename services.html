Cleaning

Have one of SEG family dental experts perform a thorough cleaning of your teeth. There can be a significant amount of plaque that builds up in difficult areas. Our hygienists can deep clean your teeth and check the health of your gums.

We suggest getting a professional cleaning once per year.

Fluoride Treatment

The fluoride treatment is typically applied after the cleaning procedure. Fluoride is a natural mineral which prevents cavities and helps rebuild the protective layer on your teeth. Fluoride Treatment can prevent or even reverse tooth damage and decay.

Talk with our talented staff to learn more.

Fillings

Fill cavities in problematic teeth. Fillings prevent further decay from cavities, and can withstand the daily wear-and-tear of chewing. Our fillings look identical to healthy teeth.

This operation has optional anesthesia. Talk with our talented staff to learn more.

Cost: 700$

Crowns

A dental crown is a type of 'dental cap' for problematic teeth. When cavities get too large or a tooth is cracked, we can apply this dental cap to prevent further damage. The crown is bonded permanently to your tooth and looks identical to a healthy tooth.

This operation has optional anesthesia. Talk with our talented staff to learn more.

Cost: 900$

Whitening

A simple whitening procedure to restore the natural color of your teeth.
Over time, the protective layer of your teeth begins to wear away and your teeth will take on a yellowish color. This whitening procedure will reverse this yellowing and brighten your teeth.

Take-home whitening kits are available for purchase in-store.

Cost: 150$

Veneers

Veneers are the ultimate dental cosmetic. A veneer is a covering that is placed on the front of any problematic teeth, changing their color and shape. Veneers hide any imperfections like tooth gaps, cracks, chips, yellowing, and more.

Veneers should only be applied to cavity-less teeth. See our Filling operation to remove cavities.

Cost: 600$

Wisdom Teeth Removal

Wisdom teeth can cause plenty of dental issues when they first appear. If your wisdom teeth are causing problems for the rest of your teeth, they can be safely removed using this procedure.
Roughly 85% of people will need to have some wisdom teeth removed at some point in their lifetime.

Talk with our experts to see if your wisdom teeth need removal. Anesthesia is required.

Cost: 1100$

Tooth Removal

The SEG Family Dental believes that we should try to keep as many natural teeth as possible. However, if teeth are irrecoverably damaged then removing the tooth entirely could be the healthiest option. It is even possible to replace the tooth with a dental implant.

Talk with our dentists to see if a tooth can be repaired instead of removed. Anesthesia is required.
